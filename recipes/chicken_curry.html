Description

Classic Indian-style chicken curry with tender chicken in a rich, creamy, spiced sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Onions, garlic, ginger, tomatoes
  • Spice blend: curry powder, cumin, turmeric, etc.
  • Cream or coconut milk

Steps

  1. Brown chicken in oil, set aside.
  2. Cook onions, garlic, ginger.
  3. Add spices, tomatoes, broth.
  4. Simmer with chicken, finish with cream.
  5. Serve with rice or naan.
