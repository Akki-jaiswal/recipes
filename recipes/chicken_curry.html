Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

This classic chicken curry recipe brings the vibrant flavors of Indian cuisine right to your kitchen. Tender pieces of chicken are simmered in a rich, aromatic sauce made with a blend of traditional spices, onions, tomatoes, and a touch of cream or coconut milk for a velvety finish. It's a comforting and hearty dish that's perfect for a family meal, best served with fluffy basmati rice or warm naan bread.

The preparation involves browning the chicken, saut√©ing the aromatics, and then slow-cooking the curry to allow the flavors to meld beautifully. It's a versatile recipe that can be adjusted to your preferred spice level, making it a favorite for both mild and adventurous palates.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or water
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream or coconut milk (optional, for richness)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • **Spice Blend:**
    • 1 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp ground coriander
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
    • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides. Remove chicken from the pot and set aside.
  2. Add chopped onion to the pot and cook until softened and translucent, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Stir in minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the spice blend (curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne, salt, and pepper) to the pot. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, until fragrant.
  5. Pour in the diced tomatoes and chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.
  6. Return the browned chicken to the pot. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. If desired, stir in heavy cream or coconut milk during the last 5 minutes of cooking for a richer curry.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Serve hot with rice or naan.
