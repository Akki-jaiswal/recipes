Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta, rich meat sauce (ragù), creamy béchamel sauce, and plenty of cheese, typically mozzarella and Parmesan. It's a hearty, comforting meal perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying on a cozy evening. The beauty of lasagna lies in the harmonious blend of textures and flavors, from the tender pasta to the savory meat and the gooey, melted cheese.

While it might seem daunting, making lasagna from scratch is a rewarding process. This recipe guides you through creating a flavorful meat sauce, assembling the layers, and baking it to golden perfection. It's a dish that tastes even better the next day!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional, to cut acidity)
  • 1.5 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds (optional, for flavor)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles (no-boil or regular, cooked al dente if regular)
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb (about 4 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. **Prepare the Meat Sauce:** In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon. Drain any excess grease.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in minced garlic and cook for 1 minute more until fragrant.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar (if using), basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 tsp salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low, cover, and let it cook for at least 30 minutes (longer is better for flavor development), stirring occasionally.
  4. **Prepare the Ricotta Mixture:** In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1/2 tsp salt, nutmeg (if using), and 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  5. **Preheat Oven & Prepare Noodles:** Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them according to package directions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.
  6. **Assemble Lasagna:** Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce (don't overlap too much).
  8. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles.
  9. Top with about a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  10. Spoon about 1.5 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella.
  11. Repeat the layers: noodles, remaining ricotta, another third of mozzarella, and more meat sauce.
  12. Finish with a final layer of noodles, the remaining meat sauce, and the last third of mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with the remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  13. **Bake:** Cover the baking dish loosely with aluminum foil (you can spray the underside of the foil with cooking spray to prevent cheese from sticking).
  14. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  15. **Rest:** Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. This allows the layers to set and prevents it from falling apart.
