Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melty mozzarella. Perfect for gatherings or cozy nights in, this hearty meal is a true comfort food classic.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or pork
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion & 2 cloves garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water
  • Spices: basil, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper
  • Ricotta cheese, 1 egg, Parmesan, mozzarella
  • 12 lasagna noodles (boiled or no-boil)

Steps

  1. Cook the meat with onion and garlic, then stir in sauces and spices. Simmer for 30 mins.
  2. Mix ricotta, egg, nutmeg, and Parmesan in a bowl.
  3. Layer: sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella. Repeat and finish with cheese.
